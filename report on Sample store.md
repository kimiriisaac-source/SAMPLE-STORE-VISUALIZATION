# Retail Business Intelligence Dashboard Report

## Project Overview.

This project focuses on creating a Power BI Retail Business Intelligence Dashboard using the Superstore dataset.

The purpose was to transform raw retail data into useful business insights by analyzing sales, profit, product performance, regional performance, and sales trends.

The dashboard helps identify business strengths, challenges, and opportunities for improvement.

## Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project were:

- Analyze overall sales, profit, and order performance
- Identify top-performing regions
- Determine the best-performing product categories
- Analyze sales trends over time
- Identify areas requiring strategic improvement
- Provide business recommendations based on data insights

# Key Findings

## Overall Business Performance

| Metric | Value |
|---|---:|
| Total Sales | $2.30M |
| Total Profit | $286K |
| Total Orders | 9,994 |

The business shows strong performance with positive sales growth and profitability.

## Regional Performance

| Region | Sales | Profit |
|---|---:|---:|
| West | $725K | $108K |
| East | $679K | $92K |
| Central | $501K | $40K |
| South | $392K | $47K |

### Insights

- West is the strongest performing region in both sales and profit.
- East is also a major contributor to business revenue.
- South and Central require more strategic attention to improve performance.

## Product Category Performance

| Category | Sales | Profit |
|---|---:|---:|
| Technology | $836K | $145K |
| Furniture | $742K | $18K |
| Office Supplies | $719K | $122K |

### Insights

- Technology is the highest-performing category, generating the most sales and profit.
- Office Supplies shows strong profitability.
- Furniture generates high sales but has low profit margins, requiring pricing and cost review.

## Sales Trend Analysis

| Year | Sales |
|---|---:|
| 2014 | $484K |
| 2015 | $471K |
| 2016 | $609K |
| 2017 | $733K |

### Insights

Sales performance improved over time, with 2017 recording the highest sales value.

The overall trend shows positive business growth.

# Business Recommendations

## Strengthen High Performing Regions

Continue investing in West and East through:

- Better customer engagement
- Improved inventory planning
- Targeted marketing strategies

## Improve Low Performing Regions

South and Central require:

- Regional promotions
- Customer acquisition strategies
- Market-specific approaches

## Focus on Technology Products

Technology should remain a priority because it provides the highest revenue and profitability.

## Improve Furniture Performance

Review:

- Discount levels
- Pricing strategies
- Operational costs

to improve profit margins.

This dashboard converts retail data into actionable business insights.

The findings can support better decisions in sales strategy, product management, and business growth.
